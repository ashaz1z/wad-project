About FitFlow

FitFlow is a personalized fitness and wellness planner designed for students. It seamlessly integrates class schedules, tailored meal plans, and mental health check-ins to support holistic well-being.

With smart planning and intuitive features, FitFlow empowers students to stay balanced, energized, and focused throughout their academic journey.

Our Values

  • Personalized Fitness Plans: AI-generated workout routines tailored to each student's level, goals, schedule and preferred activities.
  • Meal Planning:Nutritionally balanced, budget-friendly meal suggestions aligned with dietary preferences, health goals, and student affordability.
  • Class Schedule Integration: Ensures smart time management, reducing conflicts between wellness activities and academic responsibilities.
  • Mental Wellness Check-ins: Mood tracking and self-care reminders that encourage reflection and stress management.
  • Progress Dashboard: Visual summary of activity levels, meal adherence, and overall welllness trends to keep students motivated and on track.
